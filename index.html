Frequently asked questions

Who is Invoicy best for?

Owners of micro and small businesses who want cleaner accounting without building an internal finance team.

Will this add more work for my team?

No. Invoicy is designed to fit into everyday routines so people can keep working as usual.

How is Invoicy different from tools like Revolut?

Revolut is great for banking and payments. Invoicy is focused on invoice operations: collecting invoice files from your day-to-day channels, organizing them in one place, and making accountant handoff easier, no matter where the payment was made.

Is it relevant for businesses in Europe?

Yes. Invoicy is built with European small businesses and their common invoice needs in mind.
